Along with Youtube, Twitter, a social media platform, has also granted me a wealth of knowledge. Digital Marketing

So there I was googling away, youtubing away, trying to figure out what would be a field that has a low barrier to entry and that can grant me skills to have a decent lifestyle. As I descended down this rabbit hole, I discovered the field of digital marketing. In my own words, digital marketing is basically marketing through the internet. This form of marketing relies on a company's or individual's digital presence and audience. To reach the masses, entities can use a myraid of tools such as social media, email list, podcast and of course Youtube. Although these are all necessary, companies want to be seen by as many customers as possible. Their aim is to get on the first page of a google search. This is where SEO and Keywords take center stage. Keywords can be thought of as words commonly used in google searches relating to a specific service or product. SEO is known as Search Engine Optimization and optimizes your online content depending on the keywords that are used.
